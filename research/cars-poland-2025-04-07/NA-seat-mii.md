## Overview

The Seat Mii is a city car produced from 2012-2019, built on the same platform as the Volkswagen Up! and Škoda Citigo (often referred to as the "city car triplets"). It's designed as an affordable, economical urban vehicle with compact dimensions and low running costs.

**Key Characteristics:**
- 3-door or 5-door hatchback
- Front-wheel drive only
- Built in Slovakia
- Discontinued in 2019 (replaced by electric Mii in later years)
- Positioned as the sportier, more stylish alternative to the Citigo

---

## Engine Options

The Mii was available with two gasoline engine options:
- **1.0 MPI 60 HP** - Base engine, adequate for city driving
- **1.0 MPI 75 HP** - More responsive, better for highway use

Both are 1.0-liter 3-cylinder naturally aspirated engines, known for their simplicity and reliability. The 75hp version offers better performance without significant fuel consumption penalties.

**Technical notes:**
- Timing chain (not belt) - no regular replacement needed
- 5-speed manual transmission (most common)
- Automated manual (ASG) available but less reliable
- Front-wheel drive

---

## Fuel Consumption

**Official figures:**
- City: 5.6 L/100km
- Highway: 3.9 L/100km
- Combined: 4.5 L/100km

**Real-world data from owners:**
- Average: 5.5-6.5 L/100km in mixed driving
- Some owners report up to 7.4 L/100km with heavy city use
- Very economical on highway (can achieve under 4.5 L/100km)

The 1.0 3-cylinder engine is extremely fuel-efficient, making it one of the cheapest cars to run in its class.

## Common Issues & Reliability

**Reliability Rating: ABOVE AVERAGE**

The Seat Mii has proven to be a reliable city car with relatively few major issues:

**Known Problems:**
1. **Boot paintwork damage** - The boot floor has exposed paint that can be scratched by loading items (easily preventable with a protective mat)
2. **DAB radio issues** - Some units fail, check functionality before buying
3. **Gearbox/clutch** - Occasional reports of premature clutch wear (4 cases documented)
4. **Engine** - Very reliable, few reported issues (1 case documented)
5. **Interior wear** - Hard plastics can show scratches over time

**Inspection Points:**
- Check DAB radio works properly
- Inspect boot floor for paint damage
- Listen for clutch noise or slipping
- Test all electric windows and mirrors
- Check service history (should be regular, inexpensive servicing)

**Maintenance:**
- Regular servicing is inexpensive
- Parts are widely available (shared with VW/Skoda)
- No timing belt to replace (chain-driven)
- Oil changes every 15,000-20,000 km recommended

## Driving Experience

**Strengths:**
- Remarkably smooth ride quality for a small car
- Light, precise steering (easy parking)
- Smooth gear changes and clutch action
- Fun to drive in urban environments
- Feels stable and safe at all speeds
- Good visibility

**Weaknesses:**
- Can feel underpowered on highways (especially 60hp version)
- Noisy at higher speeds (typical for city cars)
- Limited rear space
- Small boot (251 liters, expands to 951L with seats down)

**Best for:** City commuting, short trips, first car, second household car
**Not ideal for:** Long highway journeys regularly, large families

## Recommendation

**VERDICT: RECOMMENDED for city use**

The Seat Mii is an excellent choice for:
- First-time drivers
- City commuters
- Second household car
- Budget-conscious buyers
- Those prioritizing low running costs

**Next Steps:**
1. Contact seller to confirm: year, exact engine power, trim level
2. Arrange inspection and test drive
3. Check service history
4. Verify DAB radio and all electrics work
5. Negotiate if any issues found (clutch, paint damage)

**Competitors to consider:**
- Škoda Citigo (same car, often cheaper)
- Volkswagen Up! (same car, higher price)
- Toyota Aygo/Peugeot 107/Citroën C1 (reliable but less refined)
- Hyundai i10 (slightly larger, also reliable)

Overall, the Seat Mii at ~9400 PLN with 96,000 km represents good value if in proper condition. Its combination of reliability, economy, and driving pleasure makes it one of the best city cars in its class.
